Coexist★

CUHK Summer School · 2024 · Mixed Media Collage

A collage series created during a summer school at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, exploring how colonial history shaped Hong Kong's visual and cultural identity. Inspired by the pirate archetype and the layered aesthetics of occupation-era fashion, the work uses color collision and figure fragmentation to visualize coexistence — of cultures, of eras, of power.
