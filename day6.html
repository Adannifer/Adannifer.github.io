Mixer Copy Kit

June 21, 2026 Copywriting · Event NYU · CrimePartner

A reusable copy kit for a fandom mixer. One prompt, one session, a full set of captions, invites, and RSVP buttons ready to paste.

Event Setting
Who
NYU students, anime and game friends, nightlife regulars. People who know what a mixer is but have never been to one they actually liked.
Where and When
Downtown NYC, summer night. After class, after work. Low-pressure by design.
Vibe
Cute chaos. Fandom-forward but not cringe. Social but not exhausting. The kind of night you show up to not knowing anyone and leave with a group chat.
Tool Used
Claude. One prompt, one round of compression, manual edits to remove anything that sounded like a marketing email.

Event Description
AFTERHOURS is a small summer mixer for NYU students, anime and gaming fans, and anyone who orbits the NYC fandom and nightlife scene. No panels. No scheduled activities. Just a good room, a summer night, and a crowd that gets weird about the same things you do.

Expect: new people, familiar fandoms, actual conversations, the kind of night you screenshot the group chat from.

Downtown NYC. Summer 2026. Free to attend, RSVP to hold your spot.
