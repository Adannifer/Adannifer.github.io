The Poster
Event Context
The Event
CrimePartner is a recurring nightlife experience in NYC built around cosplay, gaming culture, and second-world aesthetics. 3.0 escalates the format with a game night premise.
The Concept
共犯之夜 异界重启. Crime night, alternate world reboot. The tagline positions 3.0 as a new chapter with higher stakes and a darker, more immersive tone.
Date
September 26. 制服 Only dress code. 18+ venue. 2次元 CP主题.
Status
Reveal poster. Design may adjust before final publish. Two versions explored today.
Design Decisions
Gothic lettering
The chrome blackletter treatment for "CrimePartner" references underground event aesthetics. Heavy, textured, slightly distressed. Signals danger and exclusivity without being legible at a glance.
Bilingual title
共犯之夜 异界重启 sits below the English. The dual-language layout reflects the actual audience: NYC-based Chinese community in gaming and cosplay culture. Neither version translates the other.
Dark background
Near-black with subtle texture. Keeps the focus on the title treatment and date. Sets a more serious tone than CrimePartner's previous iterations.
Minimal information
Only the essentials: name, date, dress code. A reveal poster withholds. The goal is intrigue, not explanation. Details come in the next drop.
